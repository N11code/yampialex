Latest News

Underdrains (IWP & TSF)

+

Newmont Ghana Gold Limited

Yampialex Limited has started building essential underdrain systems for the Tailings Storage Facility (TSF) and In-Pit Water Pump (IWP) at the Ahafo mine owned by Newmont Ghana Gold Limited. By effectively diverting rainfall and groundwater away from operational areas to preserve embankment stability, the project seeks to improve environmental safety and water management.

Perimeter pole plinth

+

Lycopodium Minerals Limited

As part of its continuous infrastructure projects, Yampialex Limited is now building perimeter pole plinths to improve client site security and operational effectiveness. In order to assure durability and long-term performance under a variety of environmental circumstances, reinforced concrete is being used in the construction of the plinths, which are intended to provide stable foundations for perimeter lighting and security poles.

Workshop construction

+

Lycopodium Minerals Limited/ Newmont Ghana Gold limited

A contemporary workshop facility is now being built by Yampialex Limited as part of its ongoing infrastructure development initiatives for important clients in the mining and industrial sectors. Equipment maintenance, fabrication, and operational preparedness are all supported by the new workshop's architecture.
