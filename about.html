Welcome to Yampialex

Serving every customer with care

Yampialex Limited, is a fully Ghanaian-owned civil and mechanical construction company currently executing civil and piping works for Newmont Ghana Gold Limited on the Ahafo North Project

The company is led by a team of directors and technical officers with extensive experience across a wide spectrum of the construction industry. This collective expertise positions Yampialex Limited to deliver excellence in Civil Works, Piping Installation, Structural Maintenance, and Concrete Infrastructure Projects in Ghana.

Our Story

Mission Statement

To be a leading indigenous construction partner in Ghana, recognized for delivering high quality, safety driven civil and mechanical infrastructure solutions that power industrial and community growth.

Vision Statement

Yampialex Limited is committed to delivering excellence in civil and mechanical construction through a skilled workforce, innovative solutions, and a steadfast focus on safety, quality, and timely project execution. We aim to build lasting partnerships by exceeding client expectations and contributing to sustainable development across Ghana's mining and industrial sectors.

Frequently Asked Question

You May Ask

What services does Yampialex Limited provide?
Yampialex Limited offers comprehensive civil engineering and construction services including earthworks, concrete works, drainage construction, underdrains installation, plinth construction, workshop construction, and general infrastructure projects for mining, industrial, and commercial clients
Which industries do you serve?
We primarily serve the mining, oil and gas, industrial, and commercial sectors by delivering quality construction solutions that meet international standards.
Do you provide design and engineering support for projects?
Yes, Yampialex Limited collaborates with professional engineers to ensure all projects are designed and executed according to client specifications and regulatory standards.
What makes Yampialex Limited stand out from competitors?
Our commitment to quality workmanship, timely delivery, safety compliance, and client satisfaction distinguishes us in the Ghanaian construction industry.
How can I request a quotation for my project?
You can request a quotation by contacting us via our website’s Contact page, emailing us directly, or calling our office to speak with our project team.
Do you employ local labour for projects?
Yes, we prioritise employing local workers and subcontractors to support community development while ensuring all personnel meet our quality and safety standards.
What are your safety standards on site?
Safety is our top priority. We implement strict health and safety protocols in line with Ghana’s regulations and international best practices to protect our workers and clients.
Can you handle large-scale infrastructure projects?
Yes, Yampialex Limited has the expertise and resources to deliver large-scale civil construction and infrastructure projects efficiently and professionally.
Where is Yampialex Limited located?
Our head office is located in Ghana. We operate on client sites nationwide and across West Africa, depending on project requirements.
